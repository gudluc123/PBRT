How does pbrt cypto payment gateway work?

Crypto payment gateways are powered by blockchain technology. They replace the credit card with a digital wallet that requires merchants to open a merchant account to accept payment in cryptocurrencies. Thus, the transaction occurs on a decentralized peer-to-peer network where nodes exchange payment data, perform validation, and send them to the distributed ledger.
Whenever a block containing data is added to the chain, blockchain keeps the transaction details saved and ensures the transactions immutability simultaneously. Hence, a crypto payment gateway allows people to send crypto coins for a particular product or service through a decentralized payment model. Below are the steps that explain how the cryptocurrency payment gateway operates in the real world.
When a user places the order, the application records payers data (such as wallet address, etc.), encrypt it and send the details to the merchants web server via SSL connection. Using the integrated payment gateway, the merchant scans the blockchain where nodes validate and confirm the transaction.
Once confirmed, the coins are transferred to the merchant account, and the payment gateway will notify both parties about successful payment the moment verification is done on his end.The cryptocurrency payment gateway also allows the merchant to convert coins into fiat anytime at the real-time value of the currency.

What are the benefits of pbrt crypto payment gateway?

Once the cryptocurrency payment is processed through the payment gateway, it doesnt allow chargeback or refund. This feature attracts entrepreneurs who own high-risk businesses.

Anonymity
Unlike centralized payment gateways, decentralized payment gateways neither ask for registration nor KYC (know your customer) for verification purposes. Instead, it allows users to join the network anonymously. Anonymity is beneficial for vendors who sell digital assets and are concerned about privacy.
Instant processing

With no intermediaries, crypto payment processing becomes extremely fast, which takes even less than 10 seconds. It allows merchants to accept crypto and then quickly exchange them to fiat anytime.
